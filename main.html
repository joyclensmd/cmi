Alliance Application

Requirements

🩺 At least 100,000 non-botted members in the group and growing

🩺 Your group must be active

🩺 You all must be willing to help CMI.

🩺 Professional staff team

🩺 Good reputation

🩺 Your group must have a professional Discord Server

Application

🩺 What is the name of your group, link to the group, and link to the Discord Server?

🩺 Approximately how many members does your group have?

🩺 How do you think that both of our groups will benefit from this alliance?

🩺 Why did you choose CMI to ally with?

🩺 Do you know that we can pull our alliance from your group whenever we want?

🩺 Do you have 2 representatives for you group?

Make sure to include details, proper grammar, and be sure to persuade us why you would like to ally with CMI. Once your application is finished and it looks organized and professional, direct message it to one of our Hospital Directors+ or jxy#2114 (anxietysheif) We will discuss your application and get back to you as soon as possible.
